Atlantis-Visa – Your TLS appointment booking bot

🔱 Extract the whole folder anywhere on your PC.
🔱 Double-click "launch.bat".
🔱 A black window appears – that's the bot.
🔱 Chrome starts automatically. Log into TLScontact.
🔱 Go to the appointment page. The bot takes over.
🔱 Do NOT close the black window.

Appointments are booked automatically.
Screenshots are saved in %USERPROFILE%\atlantis-visa\logs\screenshots.

For any problem, contact the developer. ❤️
